jakePaste’s Top 4 Video Games

Dark Souls 3

Underneath the difficulty and frame-lag that this game has, Dark Souls 3 is an extremely fleshed out game by FromSoftware published in March of 2016. While I first started playing this game as a teenager, I still find myself coming back all the time with new builds and strategies to take on the bosses! 8/10

Crusader Kings 2

Crusader Kings 2 is a game that was created by Paradox Interactive for PC in February of 2012. A grand strategy game where you develop your family tree, fight of potential rivals to your throne, and try to expand your kingdom. While the game can often be very goofy and random sometimes, it can also be heart-wrenching when you lose one of your favorite Rulers to one of the plethoras of plagues that used to ravage Europe in medieval times. I would recommend this one only for experienced strategy gamers. 8.2/10

Fallout: New Vegas

Fallout New Vegas was developed by Obsidian Entertainment and published by Bethesda and parent company Zenimax and released in October of 2010. New Vegas is a player-driven story RPG that takes place in a post-nuclear holocaust United States, and everything’s changed. Slavers, raiders, 50’s music, a plethora of weapons, and one of, if not the best stories I’ve ever experienced in a single-player game hands down. 9/10.

Overwatch

Overwatch is a game developed by Blizzard Entertainment which was released in May of 2016. This game deserves to be in an echelon of it’s own in my opinion, but I am very biased as being good at it let me come to Mizzou and play on scholarship for the Varsity Esports program as a freshman. Overwatch is a team-based FPS with amazingly beautiful graphics, interesting characters, and plenty of abilities and strategies to try along with your friends. 10/10.
