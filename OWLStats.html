In this table, the numbers may seem close. Sparkle, or SP9RK1E, has the most amount of damage, with 1823778 being his total amount of damage dealt by the end of the season. While this, and his staggering amount of Eliminations may seem like he is the best player, it is worthwhile to realize how many Deaths he also has at the same time. So while he often does get effective damage to finish off eliminations, he is dying a lot of that time as well, which is a huge detriment to his team. On the other hand, LIP and Pelican both show significantly less damage and eliminations than Sparkle, but are also much lower on the amount of deaths they have, so they are better team players in the end.
